Steak Recipe

Home

Description

This delicious steak recipe is tender and flavorful. Seasoned with simple salt and pepper to preserve the natural flavor of the steak and topped with caramelized onion to add some additional flavor and texture. Oven roasted garlic can be placed on the steak or to the side for an additional flavor as desired. Caramelizing the onions is a bit time consuming but it is so worth it. Serve with a salad or potatoes for a dinner that is sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • 1 whole garlic clove
  • 1 whole yellow onion
  • butter or olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Sear the steak
  2. Place in the oven at 375 degrees with garlic cloves
  3. grill onoins on low in a covered pan stirring occasionally for 45 minutes
  4. Remove steak from the oven when internal temp is about 5-10 degrees below desired temp.
  5. Garnish steak with onions and garlic and juices if desired
