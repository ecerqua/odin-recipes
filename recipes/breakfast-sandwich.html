Breakfast Sandwich Recipe

Home

Description

This delicious and traditional breakfast sandwich is a great crowd pleaser or an easy solo breakfast before starting your day. I'm just covering the basics but you can expand on this sandwich with any number of add-ins like bacon, tomato and avocado.

Ingredients

  • Bread, bagel, biscuit etc.
  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • hot sauce and or ketchup

Steps

  1. Place your choice of bread in the toaster but don't start it yet.
  2. Create several thin slices of cheese.
  3. Heat a pan to medium heat and add oil once pre-heated.
  4. Crack one or two egss in the pan.
  5. When the eggs are strong enough to flip, do so, and also activate the toaster.
  6. Salt and pepper the eggs in the pan before addin gthe cheese.
  7. Allow the eggs to cook to your desired doneness and scoop onto the bread when ready.
  8. You are now ready to eat, add ketchup or hot sauce to your preference and enjoy!
